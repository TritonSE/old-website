About Us

Our Story

Effective philanthropy takes a lot of time and creativity.
That's where we come in.

In Spring of 2017, we were a group of passionate students who banded together to form Triton Software Engineering. We saw a huge problem: nonprofits had little to no resources to procure professional web and technical development services. By providing a venue for both student engineers and nonprofits to connect, we foster growth in both social good and technical expertise.

We believe that technology should be utilized to better the community. Something as simple as a sleek, updated, and easy to use website or as complicated as a mobile app to track donations to your organizations can have huge impact on any size organization. Let us let you help the community.
